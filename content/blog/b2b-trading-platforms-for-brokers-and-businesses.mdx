## 2. Scalable Infrastructure

When you are managing hundreds of clients, your infrastructure needs to be rock-solid.
*   **Multi-Tenant Architecture:** Every one of your clients gets their own secure environment.
*   **Load Balancing:** Our systems automatically scale to handle high-volume events (like the Union Budget or RBI policy days) without slowing down.
*   **Master-Child Execution:** Execute a trade in one "Master" account and have it instantly mirrored across hundreds of "Child" accounts with the correct proportional sizing.
