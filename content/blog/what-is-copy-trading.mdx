## 1. How Copy Trading Works
Copy trading (or Social Trading) allows you to "link" your account to a Master Trader's strategy.
1.  The Master Trader creates a strategy on Foxplayer.
2.  The strategy is listed on our Marketplace with a verified track record.
3.  You click "Subscribe."
4.  Whenever the Master Strategy buys or sells, the exact same trade is executed in your account instantly.

## 4. How to Choose a Strategy to Copy
1.  **Don't just look at returns:** A strategy with 500% returns might have a 90% risk of blowing up.
2.  **Look for Consistency:** A strategy that makes 5% every month is better than one that makes 50% one month and loses 40% the next.
3.  **Check the Capital Requirement:** Ensure you have enough money in your account to follow the strategy's minimum lot size.
