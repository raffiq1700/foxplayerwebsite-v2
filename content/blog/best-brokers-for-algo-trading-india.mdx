## 5. What to Look for in an Algo Broker
1.  **API Uptime:** Does the API stay connected throughout the 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM window?
2.  **Rate Limits:** How many orders can you place per second? (Crucial for scalping).
3.  **Tick Data Speed:** How fast do they stream price updates to your algo?
4.  **Customer Support:** Do they have a technical desk that understands API issues?
