## 1. What is Intraday Trading?
In an intraday trade (often called **MIS** or Margin Intraday Square-off), you do not intend to take "delivery" of the shares. You are simply betting on the price movement during the trading session. All positions must be closed before the market rings the final bell at 03:30 PM.

## 3. Best Time for Intraday
*   **09:15 – 10:30:** High Volatility. Best for "Opening Range Breakout" strategies.
*   **10:30 – 01:30:** Low Volatility. The "Sideways" market. Best for mean reversion.
*   **01:30 – 03:30:** Trend Reversal. Most "Big Moves" happen in the afternoon session.

---

## 4. The 03:20 PM Rule
Most Indian brokers will automatically square off your intraday positions around **03:20 PM**. They also charge a "penalty" fee for doing this (often ₹50 per position).
